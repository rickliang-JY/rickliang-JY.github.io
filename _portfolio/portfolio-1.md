**HikerScrolls** is a free, open-source [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/) plugin that transforms your trip notes into stunning, shareable travel journals.

## Key Features

- **3 Journal Templates** — Scrollytelling maps, scrapbook layouts, and flip-card galleries
- **AI-Powered Sketches** — Automatically generate beautiful pen-and-ink style illustrations from your travel photos
- **GPX Route Maps** — Visualize your hiking routes with interactive maps
- **Local-First** — 100% your data, no cloud required
- **One-Click Publish** — Share your journals as beautiful static pages
